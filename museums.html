The Vasa Museum

Come and experience a piece of Swedish history at the Vasa Museum in Stockholm! Our museum is home to the only preserved 17th-century ship in the world, the Vasa, which sank on its maiden voyage in 1628 and was salvaged in 1961. The ship is an impressive sight to behold, with its ornate carvings and towering masts.

But the Vasa Museum is more than just a ship. Our exhibits tell the story of the Vasa's construction, its ill-fated voyage, and its eventual recovery. You'll also learn about life in 17th-century Sweden and the fascinating engineering behind the Vasa's design.

We offer guided tours and audio guides in several languages, making the Vasa Museum accessible to visitors from around the world. And with our family-friendly exhibits and activities, there's something for everyone to enjoy.

Don't miss your chance to see this remarkable piece of history at the Vasa Museum in Stockholm. We look forward to welcoming you!

Livrustkammaren (The Royal Armoury)

Experience the rich history of the Swedish monarchy at the Livrustkammaren in Stockholm. Our museum is home to an impressive collection of royal artifacts, including clothing, weapons, carriages, and more.

You'll get up close and personal with items worn by kings and queens, as well as their horses and courtiers. Our exhibits showcase the grandeur and opulence of the royal court, while also giving visitors a glimpse into the everyday lives of the people who served them.

But the Livrustkammaren is more than just a museum. Our knowledgeable staff and engaging exhibits make history come alive. We offer guided tours and activities for visitors of all ages, making the Livrustkammaren a great destination for families, history buffs, and anyone curious about the fascinating world of royalty.

Don't miss your chance to step into the world of Swedish royalty at the Livrustkammaren in Stockholm. We can't wait to share this unique experience with you!

Medeltidsmuset (The Medieval Museum)

Step back in time and immerse yourself in the Middle Ages at the Medeltidsmuset in Stockholm. Our museum offers a fascinating look into the everyday lives of people in medieval Sweden, from peasants to nobility.

You'll explore our exhibits showcasing authentic artifacts such as weapons, clothing, and jewelry, as well as interactive displays that allow you to experience medieval life for yourself. You can even try on armor and costumes!

But the Medeltidsmuset is more than just a museum. Our knowledgeable staff and engaging exhibits make history come alive. We offer guided tours and activities for visitors of all ages, making the Medeltidsmuset a great destination for families, history buffs, and anyone curious about the fascinating world of the Middle Ages.

Don't miss your chance to step into the world of medieval Sweden at the Medeltidsmuset in Stockholm. We can't wait to share this unique experience with you!

Nordiska museet (The Nordic Museum)

Experience the rich cultural heritage of Sweden at Nordiska museet in Stockholm! Our extensive collections of traditional folk art, costumes, textiles, and household items offer a glimpse into the daily lives of Swedes throughout history. From the rustic charms of rural farmsteads to the opulence of royal palaces, our exhibits showcase the diversity and beauty of Swedish culture.

Visitors can explore recreated historic interiors, try on traditional clothing, and participate in hands-on workshops to learn more about Swedish traditions and crafts. With something for everyone, Nordiska museet is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in Swedish culture and history. Come discover the stories of our past and be inspired by our rich cultural heritage!
